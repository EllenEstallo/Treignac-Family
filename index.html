T.E.A.C.H. Artivist Retreat 2025 Participants

For four days, visionary artists and fearless artivists converged in Treignac, France (3–6 September 2025) for TEACH’s Artivist Retreat, hosted by Pat Cruz and Sangeeta Isvaren in partnership with MAVi – Moulin des Arts Vivants. Together they reimagined what it means to create, to resist, and to inspire—transforming an anti-conference into a living work of art, community, and change.
